What Is a Tip Calculator?

A tip calculator takes your bill amount and a tip percentage, then computes the tip amount and the total you owe. When dining or paying for services in a group, it also divides the total evenly among the number of people splitting the bill. This eliminates the mental math and awkward moments at the table when everyone is trying to figure out what they owe.

Tipping customs vary by country, culture, and type of service. In the United States, 15–20% is the standard range for restaurant service, with 20–25% reserved for exceptional service. In other countries, tipping may be less common or handled differently. This tool lets you set any percentage from 0 to 30% using the slider, or jump to a common preset with a single click.

The calculator updates instantly as you change any input — the bill amount, the tip percentage, or the number of people. There is no submit button to click; the results are always current and visible, making it easy to explore different scenarios before deciding on a tip.

Tips and Best Practices

A common question is whether to tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount. In most cases, tipping on the pre-tax subtotal is considered standard, though tipping on the total is also widely accepted and makes the math simpler. Either approach is fine — what matters most is that the service provider receives a fair gratuity for their work.

When splitting a bill, it is often easier to round each person's share up to the nearest dollar rather than dealing with exact cents. For example, if the total per person comes to $23.47, having everyone pay $24 is simpler and results in a slightly larger tip for the server. This tool shows you the exact figure so you can decide how to round.

If the service was exceptional, do not hesitate to go above the standard range. Conversely, if service was genuinely poor, it is acceptable to tip less — though many service workers rely on tips as a significant part of their income, so consider whether the issue was within their control before reducing the tip significantly.
