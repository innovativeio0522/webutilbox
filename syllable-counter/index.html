What is a Syllable?

A syllable is a unit of pronunciation with one vowel sound. For example:

  • "cat" = 1 syllable
  • "happy" = 2 syllables (hap-py)
  • "beautiful" = 3 syllables (beau-ti-ful)
  • "education" = 4 syllables (ed-u-ca-tion)

Haiku Syllable Pattern:

Traditional haiku follows a 5-7-5 syllable pattern:

  • Line 1: 5 syllables
  • Line 2: 7 syllables
  • Line 3: 5 syllables
