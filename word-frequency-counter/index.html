# Word Count Percentage Frequency

Word Frequency Counter - Analyze Word Usage Online

This free word frequency counter analyzes your text and shows how many times each word appears. Perfect for content writers, SEO specialists, and researchers who need to analyze keyword density and word usage patterns.

Features:

  • Word Occurrence Count: See exactly how many times each word appears
  • Percentage Analysis: View word frequency as a percentage of total words
  • Keyword Density: Identify most common words for SEO optimization
  • Common Word Filter: Option to ignore common words (the, and, is, etc.)
  • Sortable Results: Sort by word, count, or percentage
  • CSV Export: Download results for further analysis
  • Case Sensitivity: Choose case-sensitive or case-insensitive analysis

Use Cases:

  • SEO Content Analysis: Check keyword density and optimize content
  • Content Writing: Avoid word repetition and improve writing quality
  • Academic Research: Analyze text patterns in literature or documents
  • Copywriting: Ensure balanced word usage in marketing copy
  • Translation: Identify frequently used terms for glossaries

How to Use:

  1. Paste or type your text in the input box
  2. Choose your analysis options (case sensitivity, common words, min length)
  3. Click "Analyze Frequency" to see results
  4. Sort results by clicking column headers
  5. Export to CSV for further analysis if needed
