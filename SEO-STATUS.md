## 📊 MONITORING YOUR SEO

### Google Search Console (Check Weekly)
1. **Performance** - See clicks, impressions, CTR
2. **Coverage** - Check indexed pages
3. **Enhancements** - Mobile usability, Core Web Vitals
4. **Links** - See who's linking to you

### What to Look For:
- Total impressions (how many times you appear in search)
- Total clicks (how many people visit from search)
- Average position (where you rank)
- Click-through rate (CTR)

### Good Benchmarks:
- CTR: 2-5% is normal
- Average position: Under 20 is good, under 10 is great
- Impressions: Should grow weekly
