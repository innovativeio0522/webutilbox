What Is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is dummy text that has been used in the printing and typesetting industry since the 1500s. It's derived from sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 of "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (On the Ends of Good and Evil) by the Roman philosopher Cicero, written in 45 BC. The scrambled version starting with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet..." has been the industry standard placeholder text for over 500 years.

The reason designers use Lorem Ipsum instead of real text is that it has a natural distribution of letters and word lengths that mimics real language, without the distraction of readable content. When reviewing a layout, you want to focus on typography, spacing, and visual hierarchy — not on reading the actual words. Lorem Ipsum keeps the focus where it belongs.

Tips and Best Practices

When using Lorem Ipsum for design mockups, generate an amount of text that's close to what the final content will be. Using too little text can make a layout look cleaner than it will in production, while too much can make it look cluttered. If you know the approximate word count of your final content, use the "Words" mode to generate a matching amount.

For client presentations, consider using one of the fun alternatives like Hipster Ipsum or Corporate Ipsum. These are more entertaining to read during reviews and can lighten the mood in design critiques. Just make sure everyone understands it's placeholder text before the meeting starts.

If you're testing a multilingual layout, keep in mind that Lorem Ipsum is Latin-based and may not accurately represent the visual density of other languages. Chinese, Arabic, or German text, for example, can look very different in terms of character density and line breaks. For those cases, use actual sample text in the target language.
