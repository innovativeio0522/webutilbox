Color Contrast Checker - WCAG Accessibility Tool

Free WCAG color contrast checker to ensure your website colors meet accessibility standards. Test foreground and background color combinations for AA and AAA compliance.

WCAG Standards:

  • AA Normal Text: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio
  • AA Large Text: Minimum 3:1 contrast ratio (18pt+ or 14pt+ bold)
  • AAA Normal Text: Minimum 7:1 contrast ratio
  • AAA Large Text: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio

Why Contrast Matters:

  • Accessibility: Helps users with visual impairments
  • Readability: Improves text legibility for everyone
  • Legal Compliance: Required by ADA, Section 508
  • Better UX: Enhances user experience
  • SEO: Google considers accessibility in rankings

How to Use:

  • Select foreground (text) color
  • Select background color
  • View contrast ratio and compliance status
  • Adjust colors until you pass AA or AAA
  • Use preview to test readability
